My primary area of work will include the design and analysis of quantum-safe stream ciphers and block ciphers also work on cryptography Algorithms. This involves translating mathematics algorithm into functional code, ensuring the implementation's efficiency accuracy, and security.

    During my internship @ DRDO, I worked on research project called "New Hash Algorithm using 3x+1 Conjecture". We are exploring the use of mathematical algorithms and computer science techniques to find use cases of the conjectures in cryptography. The one-way function based on the 3x+1 conjecture helps build a more secure hash function; currently, we believe it is also quantum secure. We are also exploring other applications of the One-Way Function, such as random number generation and secure data transmission.
