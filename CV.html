    Projects

    Gradient Descent Algorithm on LWE Problem, Ongoing
    Pranveer Singh Institute Of Technology, Kanpur, India
    GitHub: Click

      • The Learning with Errors (LWE) problem is a fundamental problem in cryptography, where the goal is to recover a secret vector from noisy linear equations. It forms the basis of several cryptographic schemes, including lattice-based cryptography.
      • In recent years, researchers have explored the application of gradient descent algorithms to solve LWE instances. The idea is to formulate the LWE problem as an optimization problem and use gradient descent to find approximate solutions.
      • While gradient descent offers a promising approach to tackling LWE, there are several challenges and considerations to address. One challenge is the non-convex nature of the objective function in LWE, which can lead to convergence issues and getting stuck in local minima.
